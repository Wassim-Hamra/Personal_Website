Any Questions ?

Why did you make this website ?

I crafted my personal website with a clear purpose in mind: to provide a dynamic platform where I could elegantly display my projects, highlight my evolving skill set, and narrate my professional journey. In a field as competitive as data science, I aimed to set myself apart by infusing my unique style and perspective into every pixel. This venture also fueled my curiosity to delve into the realm of web development, enabling me to embrace new learning avenues and broaden my horizons beyond the confines of data analysis.

How long have you been practicing Machine Learning ?

I discovered machine learning at the beginning of 2022 after graduating from Menzah 6 High School. Since then, I have immersed myself in the data science field by studying Data Science at my university, taking online courses to sharpen my skills and increase my added value and actively participating in data related events and hackathons. I have rich experience in building machine learning solutions for various tasks. I have built models to assist in sentiment analysis, demographic clustering, and topic modelling.

Why are you interested in this domain ?

I am curious by nature. Machine learning and natural language processing are both excellent tools to understand the world better. Despite the hype over integrating AI into existing technologies, the implementation of machine learning is still in its infancy. This field is the wildwest, and I want to work with the pioneers and innovators that create new and amazing things.
